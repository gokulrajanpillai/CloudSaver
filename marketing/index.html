How it works

1. Scan locally

Choose a local, mounted, or cloud-synced folder your operating system can see.

2. Review safely

Understand large folders, duplicate candidates, and estimated image-copy savings.

3. Move reversibly

Move selected files to a review folder with a restore manifest instead of deleting blindly.

Feature highlights

Local scan engine Works with local drives, synced folders, external drives, and NAS mounts.
Duplicate review Exact candidates, verification status, confidence, and recommended keep copy.
Storage map See large folders and files before deciding what to review.
Safe review folder Selected files move with a manifest so restore stays available.
Reports Export JSON and CSV summaries for manual review or support.
Open-source trust No file uploads in the local cleanup workflow.
